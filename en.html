AlDahri dates

AlDahri Factory is now considered one of the largest date factories inside and outside the Kingdom. The development witnessed by the (AlDahri) factory exceeded expectations, as the beginning was with a manual press only, and with the development of the factory and its capabilities, its branches spread from Riyadh, Jeddah and Al-Khobar in the Kingdom to about twenty-five European, Asian and African countries, such as the United States, Canada, Australia, China, India, Egypt and other countries. .

In order for our beloved kingdom to be distinguished by the production of dates, which competes with it globally and regionally, we wanted to stand out in the midst of this huge momentum, to have steadfast steps with confidence and stability, and to build a name that will always be trusted and satisfied by all customers.
